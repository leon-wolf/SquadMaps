I need your help...

I am currently extremely busy with real life, and as much as I want to continue maintaining SquadMaps by myself (along with some community contributions), I honestly don't see there to be enough hours in the day, nor enough mental capacity to do so.
Without marking SquadMaps as 'Inactive' or 'No Longer Developing' for however long - I first thought to reach out to you guys to see if there is anyone with the free time and mental capacity to jump on board and help with development of the project. I will obviously be active enough to assist with you getting to grips with the project, answering any questions and giving a walkthrough of the code.

What is needed...

Data Miner: Working with UEViewer and conduction research to be able to acquire essential data to complete the extraction scripts (will require C++ and some Python knowledge).

Developer: Picking up from where I left off with SquadMaps v2 (will require React TypeScript knowledge).

How can I get involved...

Join the Discord Guild and drop me (mahtoid) a message.
